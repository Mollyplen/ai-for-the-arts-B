# ai-for-the-arts-B
## Mixed-Media Digital Scrapbook

**Course:** AI for the Arts & Humanities (B), University of Glasgow
**Archive Object:** 19th-Century maritime journals/photos, University of Glasgow Special Collections 

### Project Overview 
This portfolio uses human-AI collaboration to transform archival yacht journals into an accessible, multi-layered digital scrapbook that engages the public with 19th-century Scottish maritime life while critically examining whose stories survive in archives. 

### Critical Lenses:  
1. **Humanisation:** Revealing everyday emotional lives beyond grand historical narrative.
2. **Access & Inclusion:** Questioning class privilege in archival survival.
